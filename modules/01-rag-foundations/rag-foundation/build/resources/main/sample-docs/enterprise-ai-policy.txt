ENTERPRISE AI USAGE POLICY — VERSION 1.0

Section 1: Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for the responsible deployment of AI systems
within enterprise environments. All AI systems must be reliable, auditable,
and compliant with applicable regulations including the EU AI Act.

Section 2: Reliability Requirements
All production AI systems must maintain a minimum retrieval precision of 80%.
Systems must include fallback mechanisms when context confidence falls below
acceptable thresholds. Hallucination detection must be implemented before
any system is promoted to production.

Section 3: Compliance
Systems classified as high-risk under the EU AI Act must maintain complete
audit trails of all inputs, retrieved context, and generated outputs.
Technical documentation must be updated with each significant model change.
